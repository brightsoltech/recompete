our Vision

Elevating the El Paso Workforce with Recompete

The Recompete Plan will transform critical workforce challenges in El Paso and Southern New Mexico by creating opportunities and social mobility through training in the advanced manufacturing, aerospace, healthcare, and technology and artificial intelligence industries. We are aligning historic federal investments, public and philanthropic resources, and business partnerships to ensure community members have access to good-paying jobs, inclusive training programs, and comprehensive support services during training including childcare and transportation.

Diverse Industry Training

Offering specialized training in key sectors: advanced manufacturing, aerospace, healthcare, and AI, fostering opportunities and social mobility in El Paso and Southern New Mexico.

Strategic Investments & Resources

Leveraging historic federal investments and aligning public and philanthropic resources to enhance workforce development and create sustainable job opportunities.

Collaborative Business Partnerships

Working closely with local and regional businesses to align training with industry needs, ensuring a ready and skilled workforce for tomorrow.

Comprehensive Support Services

Providing extensive support during training, including childcare and transportation, to ensure accessible and inclusive learning experiences for all community members.

Strong Regional Partnerships

In collaboration with the City of El Paso, Workforce Solutions Borderplex, UTEP, and other key partners, we are committed to driving workforce excellence in the region.
